Privacy First

Your financial information stays with you.

BalancePilot is designed from the ground up around manual entry. No bank connection. No credential sharing. No data scraping. Everything stays on your device.

No Bank Login
BalancePilot never asks for bank credentials, account numbers, or login information of any kind.
No Credential Sharing
You never share access to financial accounts. BalancePilot works entirely with what you enter manually.
Manual by Design
You control exactly what information you enter and track. Nothing is imported, scraped, or synced automatically.
Stays on Device
Statement imports are processed locally. Your account data and plans never leave your iPhone.
Built for people who value control.
