Contact Us

For questions, bug reports, or feature requests, email us at support@balancepilot.net. We read every message and aim to respond as quickly as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is BalancePilot connected to my bank?

No. BalancePilot is fully manual — you enter your own balances and payments. No bank connections, no account linking.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your financial data stays on your device. We never transmit or store your account information on our servers.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete all your data by removing the app from your device. All locally stored data is deleted with the app.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

Email us at support@balancepilot.net — we read every message.
