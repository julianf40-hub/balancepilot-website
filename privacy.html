Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Overview

BalancePilot is a personal debt tracking and payoff planning tool. This policy explains what information the app and website may collect and how it is used. We keep things simple: BalancePilot does not sell your personal information and does not use it for advertising.

Information You Enter

All debt account data you add to the BalancePilot app — including account names, balances, APRs, due dates, payment amounts, and checking balances — is entered manually by you. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

How Information Is Used

Information you enter into the app is used exclusively to power the features you see: debt overviews, payoff strategy comparisons, due date timelines, payment logging, and progress tracking. It is not shared with third parties, used for analytics, or sent off your device.

Data Storage

App data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. If you delete the app, your data is removed. BalancePilot does not currently offer cloud sync or backup. You are responsible for maintaining any records you wish to keep.

Waitlist Emails

The BalancePilot website offers an optional waitlist. If you submit your email address through this form, it is collected solely to notify you when the app becomes available on the App Store. We will not send marketing emails beyond that notification, and we will not sell or share your email address with any third party.

No Financial Advice

BalancePilot is a planning and organizational tool. It is not a licensed financial advisor, broker, or credit counseling service. Nothing in the app or on this website constitutes personalized financial, legal, or tax advice. Always consult a qualified financial professional before making significant financial decisions.

Third-Party Services

  • Netlify — This website is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal hosting operations. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — The BalancePilot iOS app is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple's privacy practices apply to that distribution. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please reach out at hello@balancepilot.net.
