BalancePilot Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Overview

BalancePilot is a debt payoff planning app designed to help users organize debt accounts, balances, due dates, payoff strategies, payment progress, and related planning information. This policy explains what information the app and website may collect and how it is handled. BalancePilot does not sell your personal information and does not use it for advertising.

Information You Enter

BalancePilot is a manual-entry app. Users enter information such as account names, balances, APRs, due dates, statement dates, payment amounts, checking and cash planning amounts, protected commitments, notes, and payoff preferences. This information is entered by you and is used only to power the app's planning features.

BalancePilot does not connect to bank accounts. BalancePilot does not request bank login credentials or direct access to financial accounts of any kind. Users should avoid entering sensitive personal information beyond what is needed for debt payoff planning.

How Information Is Used

Information you enter is used to show balances, organize accounts, compare payoff strategies, calculate progress, estimate payoff timing, display due dates, and help you decide what to focus on next.

BalancePilot does not sell personal information. BalancePilot does not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or analytics purposes.

Data Storage

BalancePilot stores app data locally on your device using standard iOS app storage. BalancePilot does not currently offer cloud sync or account-based backup. If cloud features are added in a future version, this policy will be updated to explain what data is collected and how it is handled.

BalancePilot does not currently offer account login, cloud sync, or bank syncing. App data can be deleted by deleting the app from your device.

Email Contact

If you contact BalancePilot by email, your email address may be used to send App Store launch notifications, support replies, and important app-related announcements. Your email address will not be sold or shared with third parties. You can request removal at any time by contacting hello@balancepilot.net.

No Financial Advice

BalancePilot is a planning and organization tool. It does not provide personalized financial, legal, tax, or investment advice. The app and website help users organize information they already have — not to recommend specific financial products or strategies. Users are responsible for their own financial decisions and should consult a qualified professional when needed.

Third-Party Services

The following third-party services may be involved in operating this website or app:

  • GitHub Pages — This website is hosted using GitHub Pages. GitHub may process standard technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, as part of normal hosting, security, and analytics operations. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Apple App Store — When the app becomes available on the App Store, Apple may process app downloads or in-app purchases in accordance with their own privacy practices. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

BalancePilot is not intended for children under 13. The app and website are designed for users who are old enough to manage personal financial planning. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves — for example, when new features are added or hosting arrangements change. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app or website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact us at hello@balancepilot.net.
